AI Sparks a Creative Revolution

From Code to Canvas, Machines Are Learning to Dream

Vaibhavi Deshmukh

Artificial intelligence is no longer confined to data crunching and automation. Today, it’s venturing into the world of creativity — composing music, painting abstract art, and even writing poetry. This shift is redefining what it means to be a creator in the digital age.

While some fear the rise of machine-made art, others embrace it as a new form of collaboration. Artists and developers alike are exploring how algorithms can enhance human imagination, not replace it. The future of creativity might just be a duet between mind and machine.
